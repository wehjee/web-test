Enterprise AI Architecture

Build real enterprise AI with connected knowledge and structured meaning.

AI alone is not enough. Unify knowledge sources, define ontology, and activate an intelligence layer that reasons, retrieves, and orchestrates action.

01 Knowledge Source Layer
  • Connect docs, systems, CRM, events, and process data.
  • Capture metadata, lineage, and policy context.
  • Create one governed knowledge plane.
02 Ontology Layer
  • Define concepts, entities, and shared relationships.
  • Normalize meaning across business domains.
  • Version semantic models with governance.
03 Intelligence Layer
  • Apply retrieval, reasoning, and orchestration.
  • Use policy-aware agents and monitored workflows.
  • Turn structured context into reliable decisions.
04 Application Layer
  • Enable copilots, decision hubs, and workflow automation.
  • Ship reusable intelligence services to product teams.
  • Deliver measurable enterprise value.
